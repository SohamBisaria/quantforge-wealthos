# QuantForge WealthOS

## AI-Powered Financial Health Intelligence Platform

QuantForge WealthOS is an AI-powered Financial Health Intelligence Platform that helps users understand not just what they own, but how healthy and resilient their total wealth actually is.

## The Problem
Modern investors manage fragmented wealth across cash, brokerage accounts, CPF and retirement assets, property, fixed income, and digital assets. Existing platforms track balances and markets, but rarely diagnose financial health, liquidity resilience, or preparedness for shocks.

## The Solution
QuantForge WealthOS unifies listed and non-listed assets into a single dashboard and generates a proprietary Financial Health Score across five pillars:
- Diversification
- Liquidity
- Debt Health
- Goal Alignment
- Shock Resilience

The platform also includes:
- Risk & Resilience Analyzer
- Aura Wealth Coach
- Scenario Simulator
- Singapore-relevant financial planning logic

## Key Differentiation
Most platforms track wealth. QuantForge diagnoses financial health.

## Built For
Self-directed investors and advisers seeking a unified view of financial wellness across total wealth.

## Prototype Stack
- Single-file HTML/CSS/JavaScript prototype
- Interactive charts and scenario visualizations
- Simulated Singapore/global portfolio data
- AI-guided recommendation experience via Aura Wealth Coach
